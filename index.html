About Me

Hi im Myra! (Pronouced /ˈmɪrə/ or meer-uh) I am a student in the Year Up program taking courses through Seattle Central College.

I originally learned web development in middle school, but I haven't done much with it since high school. I'm currently re-learning and would love to have a fully developed site to display my work by the end of my program.

The photos shown here are photos of my girlfriend, taken by me. We met on a game in virtual reality called VRChat. Eventually I would like to have a page with my favorite photos that I can use as a sort of photography portfolio.

I was born in Utah where I lived until I was 8. My dad lost his life to leukemia in 2008, about a year before my mom finished college. My mom, my brother and I then moved to NYC as my mom obtained an internship there. We lived there for 11 years and I personally consider it the place I grew up in. While living there, my mom re-married and had two more sons, making me the eldest of 4.

I have way too many hobbies, many of which are very expensive. Skip the following paragraph if you don't want to read me go ultra nerd mode about my hobbies.

I've been rock climbing since I was 11, around 5 years of my experience have been 3 days a week. I used to dream about becoming a professional climber but never ended up committing enough time into it.
